Keep What You Own – End Unjust Property Taxation

Every West Virginian deserves true homeownership. But today, even after paying off your home, the government can still take it if you miss a tax payment. This is not freedom—this is perpetual rent to the state.

Our Demands:

  • Amend the WV Constitution to exempt fully paid, owner-occupied homes from property taxes.
  • Protect low- and fixed-income homeowners from forced displacement.
  • Rethink public funding that doesn’t rely on taxing shelter.
